about

"State of Flow,” named by a psychologist, Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi, refers to a state of being where an individual immerses completely in an activity under the right conditions. An individual experiencing flow experiences time slowing down, distractions fading away, acting instinctively and naturally, self-consciousness disappearing, activity itself becoming a reward, attention sharpening, and self-awareness dissolving.

I came across this concept and later got to experience this state of being, which inspired me to create this space, Flow.

I have always been fascinated by understanding why people behave the way they do, how the environment and genetics influence brain and behavior. This leads to the objective in creating a place to explore matters such as the meaning and purpose of life, consciousness, and human experience through philosophy, psychology and neuroscience.
