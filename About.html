About

"State of Flow" named by a psychologist Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi refers to a state of being where an individual immerses completely in an activity under the right conditions. An individual experiencing flow experience time slowing down, distractions fading away, acting instinctively and naturally, self-consciousness disappearing, activity itself becoming a reward, attention sharpening, and self-awareness dissolving.

I came across this concept and later got to experience this state of being which inspired me to create this space, Flow.

I have always been fascinated in understanding why people behave the way they do, how the environment and the genetics influence brain and behavior. Which lead to the objective in creating a place to explore on matters such as meaning and purpose of life, consciousness, and human experience through philosophy, psychology and neuroscience.
